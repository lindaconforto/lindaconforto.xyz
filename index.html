linda conforto

bio

Linda Conforto is a sound artist particularly interested in multimedia installations. Former classical flautist, she later began to explore the possibilities offered by new technologies applied to music. She is currently enrolled at the F.A. Bonporti Conservatory of Trento as a BA student in electronic music, investigating, in particular, the electronic composition and the art of multimedia installations under the guidance of Federico Ortica and programming languages for audio with Mauro Graziani. She attended masterclasses and seminars held by leading experts and artists
